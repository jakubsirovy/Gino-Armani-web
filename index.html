Who am I?

Gino Armani
Singer

Gino Armani is czech singer. He is mainly interested in latino, funk and hip hop genres. He sings his songs in Czech, English and Spanish. He cooperates with Czech and foreign artists. He has worked with artists Eddie Sender, C.Monts, Filip Gondolán and many others.
